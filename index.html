Projects:

Tarot: The Chaotic Card Based Fighting Game

Desc: "Tarot" is a 2d platform fighter where two players, fight in a first to 3 wins, on a small platform and attempt to push the other off of the platform, or hit them enough times until their health reaches 0, before they do the same to you. The gimmick of this game is, before the match, you and your opponent choose 2 "cards each" the card options are either battlefield modifiers or weapons.

Engine: Godot v4

Lang: gdScript

Mr Nibbles

Desc: "Mr Nibbles" is a game in which the player is a mouse attempting to survive throughout 3 days, this is accomplished by eating cheese, to maintain hunger, avoiding the menacing cat, and avoiding traps that get in the way. On the third day your goal is to escape the house through the newly opened window.

Engine: Unreal Engine v5

Lang: Unreal Engine's Blueprints
